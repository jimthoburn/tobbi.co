GOOD Worldwide

with the teams at Type/Code and GOOD Worldwide

Photo by The Music Center

GOOD Worldwide publishes a magazine that celebrates the positive things people are doing around the world. They also help organizations get the word out about the good they’re doing, by creating original media and facilitating brain-storming sessions with their leadership. GOOD needed a marketing website to tell their story and show their projects.

I worked alongside Erica, our designer, to create responsive prototypes that expressed a content structure and visual look for the new website. During the process, I documented the code patterns to make it easier to add new pages and features in the future.
