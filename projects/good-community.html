GOOD Community

with the team at GOOD Worldwide

The GOOD Community website, along with GOOD Magazine, celebrates individuals and organizations who are working for postive change in the world. The beautiful desktop-oriented site needed a responsive redesign.

During the project, I worked closely with our designer, Aldo, to create web prototypes which demonstrated how the new site would look at different screen sizes. We continually improved these while testing with users. In the process, I made a pattern library so anyone on the team could see the latest designs and find HTML to copy for new features.
